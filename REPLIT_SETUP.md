### 🚀 Deploy in 30 Seconds

1. **Go to Replit**: [https://replit.com](https://replit.com)
2. **Click "Create Repl"** → **"Import from GitHub"**
3. **Paste your repository URL** or drag & drop these files
4. **Click "Run"** ▶️

**That's it! Your website is live!** 🌐
